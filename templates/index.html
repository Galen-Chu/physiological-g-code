About Physiological G-Code

The Science-Philosophy Bridge

Physiological G-Code explores the fascinating parallel between two 64-state systems:

  • DNA Codons: 64 possible combinations of 4 nucleotides in triplets
  • I Ching Hexagrams: 64 combinations of 6 yin/yang lines

Both systems encode fundamental information - one for biological life, the other for philosophical wisdom. This platform enables exploration of the patterns that emerge when we translate genetic sequences into hexagrams.

Mapping Schemes

Multiple mapping methodologies are available, each revealing different patterns:

  • Binary: Direct nucleotide-to-binary-to-hexagram conversion
  • Structural: Based on molecular properties and bonds
  • AI-Induced: Machine learning discovers optimal associations
